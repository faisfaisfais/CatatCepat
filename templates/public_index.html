Get to know CatatCepat better

CatatCepat is an innovative app that designed to facilitate the process of recording and managing information quick and efficient. This app is equipped with advanced features that allow users to record audio, upload audio files, and convert them into text in real-time. Additionally, CatatCepat provides organized storage and note management, as well as a text search feature that makes it easy for users to find the information they need quickly.
